About Me

Aspiring Data Scientist

I'm a student at the University of Chicago pursuing my MS in Computer Science with a specilization in Data Analytics. I graduated with a BS in Aerospace Engineering in 2016 and discovered my love for data analysis in my spare time during my years as an engineer.

I'm deeply passionate about learning everything computer science has to offer and have spent the past year pursuing rigourous course work and self-study in pursuit of that goal. I am really enjoying my Data Analytics specialization and the courses related to machine learning, where counting techniques, probability theory, and statistical methods are prevelant. The analysis that once appeared as magic to me is now the most exciting thing I have ever studied.

Offline, I am a passionate ice hockey player, pilot, and dog lover. When I'm not at my desk, you can most likely find me at the local ice rink where I play on three different teams and occasionally help out with local youth hockey teams and skating programs.
